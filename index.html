Album Radio

Hey this is the number one open source Spotify app for album radio!

A few things you should know before you get started - Show
  1. This app can only work while you're here (and for 60 seconds after you leave)
    • You can use this to your advantage, if you don't know if you want artists related to this artist added
  2. You can only operate the radio on your own playlists, and you need to create one for me yourself along with one or more songs.
    • It's recommended to take a few songs from favorite artists to initially populate the playlist
    • You can switch between multiple playlists, just drag them to the playlist box below
  3. Album Radio deletes played songs from the start of your playlist
